Empat.io

This year during EdgeConf in London Christiann Heilmann said that the web needs more empathy. This lead to a lot of discussion about what it was that the web really needs. But the whole point in his remark is not about the web and its needs but about people and what they need to experience the web. This site is about people. They will tell you what they need. And to help you a little further this site will also give you some tips about how to help people access your information. Hopefully this will all lead to more empathy on the web.
