about people

Accessibility is...

...People

trying to do everyday stuff. Making payments and bookings, doing shopping, searching for information, staying up-to-date. When doing these things they are using what we design and build. And more than often it is difficult to use what we created. Because we tend to forget that not everybody is like us.

...Usability

People interact with the things we create in very different ways. Ways we are not familiar with. And if we don't know our products are being used we surely won't be able to shape them in a way they are usable for everybody

...Empathy

To help us understand more about our users, our visitors and sometimes even ourselves we have started creating movies about them. These are the first two. But we want to make more. To help us and everybody in our line of business understand what we are talking about when we talk about accessibility: People.

Why?

The idea for this site came to mind after I attended Edge Conf 2014 @ London. There were a lot of questions about Accessibility. And there were a lot of answers. But most of them were technical oriented. How to solve this and how to optimize for that. What seems to be missing in most discussions on accessibillity is the fact that we are talking about humans. We might even be talking about ourselves in a few years from now. Because if you understand the reasons why people are not able to use your products you might see that even you can one day find yourself in a situation where you will not be able to fulfill the simple task at hand. Because someone just plain forgot to optimize for you or the situation you are in.

Who?

I am Johan Huijkman. Currently I am employed at Q42. I have to thank my employer because he gives me all the space and time I need to build this site. Me and my wonderfull colleagues at Q42 tend to forget about accessibility every now and then. Just like almost everybody else in our line of business. But I hope by creating and sharing these movies I can help make this sort of a collective knowledge so we remember every time for whom we are building our products
